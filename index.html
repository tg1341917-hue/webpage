      GOOD TO KNOW

      FAQ

      Do my files get uploaded anywhere?

      No. Every tool runs with JavaScript already loaded in your browser. Your files are read, processed and written back to your device without a network request carrying their contents.

      What happens if I close the tab mid-process?

      Nothing is saved anywhere else, so the job is simply gone — nothing to clean up on a server, because there isn't one.

      Is there a limit on file size?

      Only whatever your browser tab can comfortably hold in memory. Very large files (500MB+) may be slow depending on your device — everything still happens locally, there's just more work for your CPU to do.
